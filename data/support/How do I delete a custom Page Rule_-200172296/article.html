How do I delete a custom Page Rule?

  1. Log into your Cloudflare account.
  2. If you have multiple websites in your account, select the website you wish to delete a page ruleÂ for at the top of the page.
  3. Select Rules > Page Rules.
  4. Click the gear icon next to the page rule you wish to delete, and then click Delete rule.
