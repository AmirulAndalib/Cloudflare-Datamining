Overview

Scrape Shield is a free app in theÂ Cloudflare dashboard that protects your site's content.

To access Scrape Shield:

  1. Log into the Cloudflare dashboard and select your account and domain.
  2. Go to Scrape Shield.

ScrapeShield has the following features:

  1. Email obfuscation: Scramble e-mail addresses for bots to prevent email spam
  2. Hotlink protection: Prevent bandwidth abuse
  3. Server side excludes: hide specific content on your site from suspicious visitors
