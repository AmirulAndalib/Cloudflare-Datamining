<p><strong>About the team</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare is redefining the way network functions are deployed at scale. Magic WAN puts the connectivity, scale, and performance of Cloudflare’s global network in front of customers’ network infrastructure, providing a single pane of glass for deploying and managing network functions like Secure Web Gateway, Next Generation Firewall, IDS/IPS, WAN optimization, and more. The Magic WAN Connector helps customers connect, steer, and shape their IP traffic. We are a team of engineers and product managers who obsess over quickly building products that solve real customer problems using innovative technologies and techniques which leverage Cloudflare’s unique architecture and global network footprint.</p>
<p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>As a software engineer on the Magic WAN Connector team, you will use your deep knowledge of Linux networking internals to design and implement distributed network functions, such as firewalls, NATs, load balancers, and QoS. You will build software systems to provide control and visibility, from user-facing APIs to distributed control systems that deploy network functions across thousands of servers. You will build internal monitoring, alerting, and management tooling to enable our operations and customer support teams to keep our global systems running at peak performance for our customers.</p>
<p>We're looking for curious, empathetic engineers of all experience levels to join our fast-growing team!</p>
<p><strong>Desirable skills, knowledge, and experience</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Expertise with Linux networking internals</li>
