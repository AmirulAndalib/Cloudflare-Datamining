<h2>Frontend Software Engineer - Network Services</h2>
<h3><strong>Locations</strong><strong><br></strong>San Francisco, CA<br>Champaign, IL<br>Austin, TX<br>Remote, US/Canada</h3>
<h3><strong>About the team</strong></h3>
<p>Cloudflare is redefining the way networks are run at scale. Our Network Services product suite puts the connectivity, scale, and performance of Cloudflare’s global network in front of customers’ network infrastructure and private networks, providing a single pane of glass for network connectivity, visibility, and operating network functions like DoS mitigation, Next-Gen Firewall, IDS/IPS, WAN optimization, and more. We are a team of engineers and product managers who obsess over quickly building products that solve real customer problems using innovative technologies and techniques which leverage Cloudflare’s unique architecture and global network footprint.</p>
<h3><strong>What you'll do</strong></h3>
<p>We are looking for experienced frontend engineers to join our team. We have growing frontend needs, so we’re looking for highly motivated individuals that are excited to join a team and work with product and content owners, designers, and other software engineers to build and scale services at Cloudflare. We work across many different product lines, so you will have the chance to interact with many different teams and learn different technologies.</p>
<h3><strong>Typical duties and responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>Work closely with designers, content owners, project managers and other software engineers to add new functionality to our Cloudflare Dashboard</li>
	<li>Build reusable components and libraries, which may be shared with other teams</li>
	<li>Build visualizations that depict large amounts of data</li>
	<li>Constantly learn new technologies and share that knowledge with others</li>
	<li>Ensure code quality by creating automated tests</li>
	<li>Mentor other frontend engineers</li>
	<li>Comfortable working both independently and as part of a team</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Education and experience</strong></h3>
