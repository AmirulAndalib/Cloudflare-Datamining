<h4>About the position</h4>
<p>We are looking for a thoughtful, detail-oriented Trust and Safety Legal Support to work with our global Trust &amp; Safety team. In this role, you will address sensitive and escalated abuse, compliance tasks, and Trust and Safety issues that affect our customers. This is a position where you will also gain familiarity with the inner workings of Cloudflare's technology and policies. As an investigator you will carefully review law enforcement requests and other legal requests seeking customer information.</p>
<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Be part of a world class globally-distributed team.</li>
	<li>Help administer efficient and compliant handling of legal requests, including but not limited to requests for information (subpoenas, court orders, etc).</li>
	<li>Be responsible for performing operations seamlessly and timely Service-Level Agreement (SLA) delivery along with maintaining a high level of quality.</li>
	<li>Partner with cross functional teams including Public Policy, Legal, and Product Engineering teams to ensure our policies and processes are carried out in a scalable fashion.</li>
	<li>Deal with sensitive content and emotionally charged escalations with tact and a calm demeanor.</li>
	<li>Participate in a rotating weekend coverage duty.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Examples of desirable skills, knowledge and experience</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>You have minimum 6 years of legal support experience at an Internet Service Provider, hosting provider, or law firm.</li>
	<li>Demonstrate working knowledge of DNS and how the Internet works.</li>
	<li>You’ve worked with legal teams on document production requests.</li>
	<li>You’ve interacted with law enforcement officials and agencies.</li>
	<li>Experience managing sensitive content and emotionally charged escalations with tact and a calm demeanor.</li>
	<li>You have a strong anti-abuse and/or support escalation background.</li>
	<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, attention to detail and ability to manage multiple projects and tasks.</li>
</ul>
<p>Bonus Points:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Experience with the global legal considerations related to legal requests seeking customer information.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Experience in compliance, sanctions, and similar regulatory regimes.</li>
</ul>
