<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<p>The Cash Manager will oversee Cash Application functions and team. This role helps to manage and facilitate prompt, correct, and complete bank reconciliations, payment<br>applications, billing and collection of Cloudflare receivables to support preparation of accurate financial statements, compliance with customer contracts, and optimization of<br>cash flow. Additionally, this position participates in certain regulatory compliance activities including sales tax assessment and reporting, and unclaimed property remittance.</p>
<ul>
	<li>Billing and Accounts Receivable</li>
	<li>Bank Reconciliations</li>
	<li>Payment processing</li>
	<li>Monthly Reporting</li>
	<li>Oversee responses to billing and A/R inquiries in a manner that cultivates a culture of exemplary customer service both internal and external</li>
	<li>Participate in month-end close processes associated with Accounts Receivable to support timely and accurate preparation and delivery of financial statements</li>
	<li>Execute processes for managing and refunding customer credit balances in a timely manner, partnering with Accounts Payable to escalate or resolve any A/R related unclaimed property issues.</li>
	<li>Establish and maintain Cloudflare credit policies</li>
	<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams ; Contracts,RevOps, SalesOps,</li>
	<li>Customer Success, Tax, Legal, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Systems</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Oversee maintenance of customer account information within Billing System(s) to ensure up-to-date address, terms, tax, and contact information
		<ul>

			<li>High Radius</li>
			<li>Stripe</li>
			<li>Sales Force</li>
			<li>Jira</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li>Uphold the current policies and procedures for Billing and A/R</li>
	<li>Support overall organization’s SOX compliance requirements through adherence to key controls and timely completion of reporting.</li>
	<li>Create and maintain a credit policy for Cloudflare</li>
</ul>

	<li>Advanced Microsoft Office and G-Suite skills.</li>
	<li>NetSuite, Salesforce, High Radius, Stripe experience preferred</li>
