<h4>About the position</h4>
<p>Finance process transformation manager cultivates partnerships between Finance and the business and spearheads key initiatives at the company level. This Manager will help socialize a clear vision for the future state operating model, advocating a case for change, and driving alignment and organization through active project management.</p>
<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Partner with all senior Finance Leaders to align with the business strategy</li>
	<li>Partner with Finance teams to modernize processes through digital finance transformation</li>
	<li>Establish targets, program and project prioritization, and speed to benefit realization</li>
	<li>Provide oversight, and control of program portfolios established to manage finance improvement efforts</li>
	<li>Partner with business leads and sponsors to determine appropriate ways to navigate complex issues and change management agendas that Finance can help shape</li>
	<li>Partner with Finance Business Systems to help better leverage or implement digital technologies to improve operational effectiveness, compliance and reporting, incorporating digital enablers for end-to-end finance processes (such as procure-to-pay, order-to-cash, record-to-report, hire-to-retire) by assessing challenges/needs/gaps</li>
	<li>Diagnose root cause and recommend end-to-end solutions that use digital workflows and tools and harness the power of robotic process automation, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to reinvent and accelerate work</li>
	<li>Work with all digital finance transformation partners to ensure prioritization and execution of project plan aligns with the Company&amp;#39;s goals</li>
	<li>Participate in process development and improvement discussions to help determine performance metrics</li>
	<li>Formulate a communication strategy to keep participants / users informed about the direction of the team’s transformation efforts</li>
	<li>Coordinate with the PMO to help with the management, tracking and communication status of workstream efforts, program risks/issues, and progress towards strategic goals</li>
	<li>Orchestrate the creation of comprehensive playbooks and procedures to ensure clear documentation for operational and control purposes</li>
	<li>Maintain an understanding of advancements in technology and innovations that are<br>happening across digital, analytics, the CFO ecosystem, and broader marketplace</li>
	<li>Promote a culture of high performance and continuous improvement and innovation that values learning and a commitment to quality</li>
	<li>Be a change ambassador across the Global Finance network, infusing learning, creating forum to share best practices and develop skills in change management</li>
	<li>Provide ad-hoc strategic, analytical and presentation support for a variety of executive audiences</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Examples of desirable skills, knowledge and experience</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, or Economics</li>
	<li>8+ years experience working in Finance organizations or advising on Finance transformation, process improvements, financial planning redesign, service delivery model design and implementation, and other essential elements of the Finance team</li>
	<li>Proven ability to work with diverse stakeholders, manage competing agendas and priorities, and translate complex information across functions</li>
	<li>Experience with Finance operating models and supporting organization structures; In- depth knowledge of finance process leading practices</li>
	<li>Understanding of finance and ERP technologies; Alteryx and Robotic Process</li>
	<li>Automation subject matter expertise is a bonus!</li>
	<li>Experience with requirements gathering, design and implementation of financial systems</li>
	<li>Experience in continuous improvement programs, root cause analysis, and project management</li>
	<li>Outstanding integrity, initiative, creativity, and passion</li>
	<li>Record of career progression with increasing levels of responsibility</li>
	<li>Looking for someone who is a self-starter, willing to bring ideas to the table with proven success in execution</li>
</ul>
