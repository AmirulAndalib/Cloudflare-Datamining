<h4>About the department</h4>
<p>Cloudflare is seeking a Technical Accounting Manager to join our Accounting team. Our team consists of experienced, motivated, and self-sufficient high performers. The ideal candidate will have strong technical skills, attention to detail and be able to work in a fast-paced high-volume environment.</p>
<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<p>The Technical Accounting Manager is responsible for providing accounting guidance for complex or unusual transactions such as acquisitions/divestitures, legal transactions, impairment, investments, and other non-routine complex business transactions. Responsibilities include critically evaluating applicability of new accounting literature to Cloudflare, preparing quarterly and ad-hoc technical accounting memos, maintaining effective internal controls and ensuring accounting activities are both regulatory and U.S. GAAP compliant.&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
	<li>Research authoritative literature and provide technical accounting guidance on complex transactions such as acquisitions/divestitures, legal transactions, impairment, investments, including other non-routine complex business transactions</li>
	<li>Evaluate applicability of new accounting literature to Cloudflare and ensure accounting activities are both regulatory and U.S. GAAP compliant</li>
	<li>Perform quarterly review of goodwill and intangible impairment including year-end test for impairment</li>
	<li>Manage actuarial valuation of pensions and ensure U.S. GAAP compliance in accordance with ASC 715-30</li>
	<li>Review and monitor SAB99 analysis to evaluate errors in financial statements using both the "iron curtain" method and "rollover" method</li>
	<li>Review accounting conventions to ensure Cloudflare's non-GAAP policies would not have a significant effect to any one period on a quantitative basis</li>
	<li>Prepare segment analysis to ensure compliance with requirements regarding consistency between internal and external reporting of reportable operating segments</li>
	<li>Prepare/review timely, high quality technical accounting memos on all key technical accounting matters for submission to the independent auditor</li>
	<li>Manage and act as liaison in Company-wide initiatives and cross-functional projects that directly impact accounting area to ensure U.S. GAAP compliance. Examples include implementation of new accounting policies, due diligence and PPA entries on acquisitions and other significant business transactions such as divestitures, Company spin-offs, restructuring, debt offerings, complex capitalization projects, and other significant complex transactions</li>
	<li>Assist external reporting team with complex regulatory reporting matters.</li>
	<li>Providing detail support to the outside auditors and other consultants</li>
	<li>Manage multiple tasks under tight deadlines with strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
</ul>
