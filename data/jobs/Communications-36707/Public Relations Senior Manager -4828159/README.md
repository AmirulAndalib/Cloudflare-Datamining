<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cloudflare is looking for a Sr. Public Relations Manager to join our team. In this role, you will be involved in every aspect of Cloudflare’s overall communications program with a special focus on our security offerings, thought leadership, and technology innovation.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You’ll work with many teams at Cloudflare, helping to find and build out stories that help move the needle for us as a brand and as a company. You will drive communications for pivotal product launches and security announcements to educate the market on Cloudflare’s technology. You’re an expert storyteller and thrive on telling compelling security stories to vertical and mainstream audiences. You’re used to working with technical teams and able to break down complex technologies and distill them into high-impact PR campaigns and coverage. In this role, you’ll have the opportunity to build strong relationships with a range of product and engineering leaders and will work closely with our executives to craft bylines, press releases, speaking submissions/talks, and media material. You’ll also lead these activities with our global PR agencies. As a rapidly-growing public company, this is an exciting opportunity to join an expanding Communications team and to help continue building Cloudflare’s story.</span></p>
