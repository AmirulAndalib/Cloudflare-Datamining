<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare is a network which truly spans the globe, on a mission to make the internet better, safer, and more powerful everyday.&nbsp; As an engineer on our team, you will be responsible for the infrastructure that defines how we run and maintain applications on the Edge and get it to 275+ cities around the world.</p>
<p>How can you safely and quickly&nbsp;<a class="external-link" href="https://www.cloudflare.com/products/zero-trust/browser-isolation/">run a customer's entire browser at the Edge</a>? Come find out.</p>
<p>What about collocating customer&nbsp;<a class="external-link" href="https://workers.cloudflare.com/">workers</a>,&nbsp;<a class="external-link" href="https://blog.cloudflare.com/introducing-d1/">databases</a>, and&nbsp;<a class="external-link" href="https://www.cloudflare.com/lp/pg-r2/?utm_source=google&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=PAYGO_%7c_Global_-_ENG_Native_%7c_ENG_%7c_G_%7c_Search_%7c_Applications_%7c_Premium_%7c_CORE&amp;utm_content=&amp;utm_term=cloudflare%20r2&amp;campaignid=18389070231&amp;adgroupid=142673049738&amp;creativeid=623128479598&amp;&amp;_bt=623128479598&amp;_bk=cloudflare%20r2&amp;_bm=e&amp;_bn=g&amp;_bg=142673049738&amp;_placement=&amp;_target=&amp;_loc=9060228&amp;_dv=c&amp;awsearchcpc=1&amp;gclid=Cj0KCQiAg_KbBhDLARIsANx7wAzKFH3DnFYM_CSOtU_KnzWMnwpQeyKGNr1L7bsjM7m5Z9YWWr1JRHoaApp-EALw_wcB&amp;gclsrc=aw.ds">object storage</a>? Come help build it!</p>
<p>So few engineers have the opportunity to work on these fundamental cloud platform challenges. We are building scalable, secure and fast platforms. Those aren't just buzzwords; each is a pillar of what our team is building. And we are doing it on a global scale - from huge datacenters in London to three nodes in Curaçao.</p>
<p><strong>Skills, Knowledge and Experience</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for engineers with battle-tested experience in global scheduling and multi-VM platforms who want to build something new and transformative for the company that&nbsp;<a class="external-link" href="https://w3techs.com/technologies/details/cn-cloudflare">nearly 20% of the internet</a>&nbsp;already depends on.</p>
<p>Here are the technologies and problems we face today</p>
<ul>
	<li>Inter-datacenter (global) scheduling - think Kubernetes on a global scale, built on Workers and Durable Objects</li>
	<li>Inner-datacenter scheduling with HashiCorp Nomad</li>
	<li>Machine level virtualization and security with Firecracker and gVisor</li>
	<li>Machine level scheduling and process management with containerd, cgroups, and KVM - memory sharing, CPU throttling, etc</li>
	<li>Network and routing in Linux</li>
	<li>High level of proficiency with Linux is a must</li>
	<li>Strong familiarity with Golang or the ability to acquire it quickly</li>
	<li>Experience with Rust (Firecracker &amp; rust-vmm) valuable but not necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>If all or most of this sounds interesting to you, and you're ready to build and be challenged with a great team working on new platforms, please apply</p>
