<h4>About the department</h4>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cloudflare is one of the world’s largest networks. With direct connections to nearly every service provider and cloud provider, the Cloudflare network can reach 95% of the world’s population within approximately 50 ms. Everything we build sits on our network. How our network is architected differentiates us and gives Cloudflare a distinct competitive advantage.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Cloudflare Network Finance Team partners directly with management team leaders to build and finance our network, streamline our operations and manage deal economics. Members of this team have a deep understanding of all aspects of Cloudflare’s business and play an important role in aligning goals, setting targets as well as bringing visibility and predictability to our financials. Areas of coverage intersect with critical components of our financial statements: revenue and gross margin (pricing and deal economics), cost of revenue/gross margin (network operations and innovation), capex (building the network).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Team members possess excellent analytical, critical reasoning, interpersonal, communication, influencing and leadership skills and are passionate about both problem-solving as well as building high-quality repeatable processes that scale with our rapid growth. Our role extends beyond analyzing and reporting; we take pride in helping to design and implement solutions to the different challenges that can arise in our high-growth environment.</span></p>
