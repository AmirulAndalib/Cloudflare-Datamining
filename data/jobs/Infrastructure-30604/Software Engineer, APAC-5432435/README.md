<p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>You will work as part of our core engineering team in APAC which builds the Cloudflare infrastructure running in 200+ cities around the world. Your code will impact upwards of 2.8 billion people/month, as well over 10% of all internet connections which pass through our servers. Your work will focus on building robust, secure and high performance distributed systems. We are a small engineering team operating at a massive scale: our network spans to more than 300 cities in over 100 countries, including approximately 30 locations in Mainland China and interconnects with over 12,500 networks globally, including major ISPs, cloud services, and enterprises with 209 Tbps of network capacity&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>You will be supported by colleagues, and collaborate with other teams around the company. Our global Infrastructure engineering teams include:</p>
<p><strong><em>Provisioning tools</em></strong></p>
<p>Build tooling/automation and infrastructure services that help Data Center Engineering teams at Cloudflare enable nodes in production with minimal interaction, contributing towards Zero Touch Provisioning. Our team works with all of the engineering teams at Cloudflare and plays a critical role to reduce node time to production. Some examples on what we built you can find it <a href="https://blog.cloudflare.com/automating-data-center-expansions-with-airflow/">here</a></p>
<p><strong><em>APIs</em></strong></p>
<p>Builds the public facing APIs that power the Cloudflare infrastructure, granular access control policies for adapting to different workflows, enabling users to perform a variety of tasks, these foundational models have a far reaching impact.</p>
