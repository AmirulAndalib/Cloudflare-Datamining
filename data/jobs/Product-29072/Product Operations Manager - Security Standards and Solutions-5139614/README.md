<h4><strong>About the Role</strong></h4>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re looking for a Product Operations Manager who will manage key cross-product and cross-company solutions including FedRAMP and other Government and Public Sector data-sovereignty, security certification, privacy, standards and compliance initiatives. You will work across many teams at Cloudflare including Product, Engineering, Security, IT, Compliance, Sales, Marketing, Privacy, and Legal. You will help translate requirements into product features, solution architectures, infrastructure capabilities, process and compliance standards. You will use your deep technical and collaboration skills along with your hands-on approach to achieve timely results across multiple major projects.&nbsp; Your role will require you to act as a cross-product Product Manager collaborating with individual Product Managers and Engineering Managers while also acting as a cross-organization leader and subject matter expert.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Define and prioritize the cross-product features and functionality required to ensure Cloudflare meets and maintains the requirements of major cross-product and cross-company compliance and security certification initiatives. </span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Lead and guide product managers in planning features that meet Public Sector security standards.Work with Go-to-Market teams including Pricing and Licensing, Product Marketing, Sales, Customer Support and others to bring Public Sector security certification offerings to market.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Lead Cloudflare’s&nbsp; product and solution compliance for FedRAMP .</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Act as a leader in all globalPublic Sector security standards, data sovereignty and compliance projects at Cloudflare taking a hands-on approach, collaborating with cross-functional teams.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Engage with customers, prospects, and internal stakeholders to understand core capabilities required and develop plans to meet those requirements.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Engage directly with customers to facilitate an understanding of how using Cloudflare products will help them stay in compliance with the security certification and compliance requirements applicable to them.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Help Product and Engineering teams weigh different options to meet the requirements of these initiatives and understand the tradeoffs of each.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Act as a subject matter expert in the product and engineering capabilities of these projects, participate in customer, internal and external meetings/communications.</li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;">Own the Public Sector security compliance product roadmap and help customers fit Cloudflare’s roadmap into their enterprise roadmaps.</li>
</ul>
