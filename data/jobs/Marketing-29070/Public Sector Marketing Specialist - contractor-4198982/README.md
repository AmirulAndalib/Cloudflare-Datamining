<p>The Public Sector Marketing Specialist is a key player in driving State and Local government, Federal, Higher Education and K-12 marketing initiatives. Alongside the Sr. Public Sector Marketing Manager, this position works closely with our marketing, sales, and supporting teams to drive industry business goals and objectives. The Public Sector Marketing Specialist supports the development and execution of the Public Sector marketing plan to influence overall ROI, pipeline, and vertical growth.&nbsp;</p>

	<li>Support marketing vertical strategies, with the ability to document and report on quarterly marketing plans, execution and results</li>
	<li>Execute&nbsp;end-to-end in-person and virtual pre/post event logistics (webinars, sponsored programs, trade shows, conferences)&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Work closely with sales leadership and AE/BDR teams to ensure alignment on strategy, targets, reporting views, and program objectives and results</li>
