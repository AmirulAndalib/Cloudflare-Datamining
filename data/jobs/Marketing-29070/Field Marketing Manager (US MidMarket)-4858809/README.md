<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Americas Marketing Team is looking for an experienced, enthusiastic and highly-motivated Field Marketing Manager who will be responsible for positioning Cloudflare in the new </span><strong>Digital Native Accounts Segment of the Mid Market segment in the US.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will build the marketing plan for the region, integrating field events, account-based campaigns and programs in order to grow Cloudflare’s customer base in the Digital Native segment.&nbsp; You have a natural inclination to measure the effectiveness of marketing activities, show strong project management skills and are able to think strategically while orchestrating many moving parts. The programs you oversee will ultimately drive demand and pipeline in this new segment of the Americas region.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The accounts in the </span><strong>Digital Native segment</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> are high-growth, venture-backed companies that typically buy and procure directly from vendors. They are also referred to as start-ups “born on the web”.&nbsp; Some of the main industries include Computer Software, Internet, Media, Cryptocurrency/Blockchain, and other Web3 companies.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This segment includes two groups:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Digital Native </span><strong>Named</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Accounts: Technology companies, Software, SaaS, Internet, Media, Cryptos, etc. Accounts in the above industries established after 2000, or additional industries (such as FinTech or HealthTech)&nbsp; with funding in the last 10 years. Employee size: </span><strong>300 - 5000</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Digital Native </span><strong>Emerging</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Accounts: Technology companies, Software, SaaS, Internet, Media, Cryptos, etc. Accounts in the above industries established after 2000, or additional industries (such as FInTech or HealthTech)&nbsp; with funding in the last 10 years. <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will work closely with the Digital Native sales team to understand challenges, opportunities and to align with their quarterly goals. You will also partner with the Midmarket Account-Based Marketing team to build an integrated strategy that orchestrates marketing and sales touchpoints, and offers a better experience for our prospects and customers.&nbsp;</span></p>
