<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare is looking for a Senior Manager, Global Partner Demand Programs — a vibrant, inventive, and data-driven individual adept at crafting scalable partner-focused demand generation campaigns. Someone with a proven talent for building nurturing programs, maximizing partner campaign engagement, and consistently generating new partner-sourced pipelines.</p>
<p>You will own the demand generation program strategy and execution for the Cloudflare Partner ecosystem globally, working closely with our Campaigns and Regional Field Marketing teams to leverage best-performing campaigns and optimize them for partner pipeline delivery. You will conceive, launch, manage, and optimize demand generation programs for partner business acceleration that span a variety of online channels – display, retargeting, email, direct mail, social, webinars, content syndication, to name a few. You have a natural inclination to measure the effectiveness of marketing activities, strong project management skills, and the ability to think strategically and orchestrate many moving parts.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Develop and execute a strategy for thru-channel marketing programs, including repeatable partner marketing programs, including social, email and webcast campaigns leveraging our marketing partner automation portal to achieve Cloudflare's partner-sourced pipeline and revenue objectives.</li>
	<li>Design co-brandable partner to-go campaigns and downloadable assets across multiple channels (email, display, paid social, webinars, etc.)&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Construct a sponsorship strategy and framework designed to amplify reach, foster participation, and ensure the inclusion of our partners in Cloudflare-driven events and activities, i.e., Cloudflare TV.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Act as a point person between marketing and our partner team to drive demand generation program awareness and activation.</li>
	<li>Build effective reports to track partner campaign activation, performance, and ROI&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Develop a framework and content to scale through partner email nurture programs to help partners push qualified leads further down the funnel for conversion.</li>
	<li>Work with cross-functional team members (Field Marketing, Partner Sales, Design, MarTech, Product Marketing, etc.) to support orchestrating high-impact integrated online marketing campaigns.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Requirements</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Minimum 5 years of proven experience in B2B marketing (enterprise software, SaaS, Security, etc.)</li>
	<li>Minimum 3 years hands-on experience building through-partner campaigns with demonstrated strong partner-sourced pipeline delivery</li>
	<li>Deep understanding of different partner types and their routes to revenue&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Good understanding of account based marketing and all digital marketing channels</li>
	<li>Familiarity with Salesforce, Marketo, HubSpot, Eloqua (or other marketing automation tools)</li>
	<li>In this highly visible role, you must be comfortable with reporting and communicating campaign efforts and impact across the organization</li>
	<li>Analytically inclined, exceptional organizational skills, with rigorous attention to detail</li>
	<li>Ability to work effectively, influence managing a wide set of stakeholders in a fast-paced and ever-changing environment with tight deadlines</li>
	<li>Excellent written and stellar presentation skills</li>
	<li>Extreme curiosity.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Bonus points</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Familiar with channel marketing technology and platforms, including Through Channel Marketing Automation (TCMA) and partner portals&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Prior demand generation experience in a high growth company in the SaaS, security, or network space, combined with knowledge of how the Internet works is a plus.</li>
</ul>
