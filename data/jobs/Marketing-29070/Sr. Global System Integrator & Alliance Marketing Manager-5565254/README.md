<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>As the Sr. Global System Integrator &amp; Alliance Marketing Manager, will be responsible for managing marketing initiatives, campaigns, and content creation for Cloudflare's highly strategic and quickly expanding set of Global System Integrators (GSI) and key Alliance partners. You will apply your deep, cross-functional marketing skill set and expertise in cloud stack technologies to grow global engagement, pipeline, and revenue with this set of critical partners, i.e., Accenture, Rackspace, etc. This is a highly visible role across the company and will require you to work closely with senior stakeholders from Partner Sales, Regional Sales, Operations, Product Management, Product Marketing, and other organizations to deliver compelling content, engaging partner experiences, and high-performing lead-generation campaigns.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Position the company's products and services effectively in collaboration with system integrators and alliance partners. Ensure consistent branding and messaging across all joint marketing initiatives.</li>
	<li>Develop strong marketing interlock within the partner organization.</li>
	<li>Oversee the creation of marketing content such as case studies, whitepapers, blog posts, and social media content that highlight the strengths of the partnerships and integrations.</li>
	<li>Build and manage co-marketing campaigns with partners to achieve target outcomes across the sales funnel (awareness - consideration - close), including hosted or third-party events, webinars, and digital campaigns, as well as the tracking and reporting of outcomes of those campaigns.</li>
	<li>Lead partner-facing and through-partner enablement, sales, and marketing content development in collaboration enablement and SMEs throughout Cloudflare.</li>
	<li>Own a communications framework and cadence for GSIs and Alliances, including messaging, news articles, and recurring business review and feedback mechanisms.</li>
	<li>Monitor and analyze the performance of marketing campaigns and partnerships. Utilize metrics and KPIs to measure return on investment and make data-driven decisions for future marketing strategies.</li>
	<li>Manage the marketing budget allocated for system integrator and alliance activities. Ensure efficient use of resources to maximize marketing impact.</li>
	<li>Maintain regular communication with internal stakeholders and external partners. Provide regular reports on campaign progress, market insights, and partnership developments.</li>
</ul>
