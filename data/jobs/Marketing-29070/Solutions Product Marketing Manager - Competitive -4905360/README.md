<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Solutions Product Marketing Manager - Competitive&nbsp;</span></p>

Think SASE, Zero Trust, and Security Operations Center (SOC). You will work with colleagues in sales demand generation, business development and product management to create compelling content that drives measurable success for our business. You will develop strategic marketing plans and evangelize solution messages to prospects, customers, sellers and partners.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will understand customer needs, dig into product details, and elevate our messaging to deliver solution-oriented, breakthrough initiatives. You will enjoy this role if you like working with fast-paced teams and having autonomy to drive your initiatives.&nbsp;</span></p>
