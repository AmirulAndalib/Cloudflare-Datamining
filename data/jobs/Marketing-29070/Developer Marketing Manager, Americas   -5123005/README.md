<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<p>Cloudflare is seeking a dynamic and innovative Developer Marketing Lead to join the Americas marketing team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies and initiatives targeted specifically at developers. Your primary goal will be to engage and empower developers to adopt and utilize Cloudflare products and services. You will work closely with cross-functional teams, including global developer marketing, product management, engineering, and sales, to drive developer adoption, increase product awareness, and ultimately contribute to Cloudflare’s mission to build a better Internet.&nbsp;</p>
<h4>Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Develop and execute comprehensive marketing strategies to target and engage developers around Cloudflare’s “Workers” development platform, including creating compelling messaging, campaigns, and content across various channels.</li>
	<li>Understand developer personas, their needs, pain points, and motivations to develop effective marketing initiatives.</li>
	<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with developer communities, influencers, and key stakeholders.</li>
	<li>Partner with the global developer marketing lead to design engagement strategies and programs</li>
	<li>Establish &amp; leverage partnerships with complementary technology providers</li>
	<li>Influence and champion content and program needs to engage the developer audience</li>
	<li>Organize and participate in industry events, conferences, and meetups to promote Cloudflare products and services to developers.</li>
	<li>Drive developer engagement and adoption of Workers through online and offline activities, such as hackathons, workshops, meet-ups, hands-on labs, etc</li>
	<li>Conduct market research and competitive analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and best practices for developer marketing.</li>
	<li>Track, analyze, and report on the performance of developer marketing initiatives, including KPIs, ROI, and engagement metrics.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Required Skills &amp; Experience</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Minimum 5 years of experience managing developer programs</li>
	<li>Established relationships with developer oriented media properties such as Slashdot, DevOps.com, StackOverflow, etc.</li>
	<li>Ability to effectively influence both internal and external stakeholders&nbsp;</li>
	<li>A passion for Cloudflare’s mission and the success of our customers, partners, and community.</li>
	<li>Excellent planning, project management, prioritization skills</li>
	<li>Strong data-management skills and&nbsp;analytical skills to analyze campaign effectiveness and ROI</li>
	<li>Deep curiosity, excellent listening and empathy skills</li>
	<li>Positive and flexible attitude for working in a fast-paced environment and ability to build strong relationships across various departments and functions</li>
</ul>
