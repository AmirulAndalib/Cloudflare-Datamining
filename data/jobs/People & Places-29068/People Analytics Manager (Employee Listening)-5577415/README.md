<h4>About the Role</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Support the design and execution of a holistic Employee Listening measurement strategy, including design and administration of lifecycle and ad hoc surveys and integration of insights across the talent lifecycle.</li>
	<li>Partner with the global People Leadership Team to design and deliver insights that will inform strategy and help execute on our talent initiatives.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Partner with the global Business Partner team to design and deliver quarterly insights that will inform business strategy and help execute on talent initiatives.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Demonstrate thought leadership on current research and innovative solutions to assessing and measuring the talent lifecycle, from pre-hire to exit.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Collaborate proactively with People Team Leadership (e.g, TA, L&amp;D) and other key stakeholders to identify critical people issues and to support delivery of insights and analytics.</li>
	<li>Review, monitor, and communicate results effectively for impact and provide follow-up insights in a timely manner.</li>
	<li>Design and deliver integrated talent insights via Tableau.</li>
	<li>Support other People Analytics initiatives and global People Team/business initiatives, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Skills/Experience</h4>
<ul>
	<li>Proven knowledge of and experience with end-to-end talent lifecycle measurement, specifically related to talent assessment, delivery of survey insights, and execution of talent priorities.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with a strong attention to detail.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Ability to integrate, analyze, and effectively communicate insights to all levels of the organization.</li>
	<li>Experience working directly with leadership at all levels and functions to define and implement solutions.</li>
	<li>Demonstrated ability to create creative and compelling insights that are leveraged by multiple teams in an asynchronous setting.</li>
	<li>Demonstrated ability defining new datasets, linking disparate forms of data, and/or creatively using existing data to enable decision making for multiple teams.</li>
	<li>Intermediate skill with Tableau and experience integrating large datasets via BigQuery.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Requirements</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>7+ years of relevant experience, preferably in a People Analytics/Talent role or equivalent at a global company.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Advanced analytical skills with demonstrated ability synthesizing and interpreting employee data.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Advanced degree in Industrial/Organizational Psychology, Organizational Behavior, Human Resources, Business Analytics, or relevant field preferred.</li>
	<li>Demonstrated track record and exceptional judgment handling sensitive data.</li>
	<li>Proficiency with Perceptyx, Workday, Google Suite, MS Excel, Tableau, and BigQuery/SQL. Proficiency with other ATS and survey vendors recommended.</li>
</ul>
